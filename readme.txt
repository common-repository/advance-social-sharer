Add social button to your wordpress website for share your post to social network such Facebook,LinkedIn,Twitter and Google.

== Description ==

If you are looking to boost your online presence by sharing your content with a wider audience, this social media plugin for WordPress is all you need. You can add Google, Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn WordPress social share buttons to your posts. Advance Social Sharer is your ultimate plugin that enables improved reach to your target customers across different social media platforms. 

Requires WordPress 3.0 and PHP 5.

<strong>Features:</strong>
<ul>
	<li>One-click and integrated sharing of content over social media platforms. </li>
	<li>Instant content sharing for viewers on their preferred social media platforms.</li>
	<li>Tailored and flexible placement of the social sharing plugin buttons as per your website's design.</li>
	<li>Responsive and optimized plugin for mobile devices.</li>
	<li>User-friendly configuration and customization options.</li>
	<li>Overall control of the content being placed.</li>
</ul>

== Installation==


1. Upload `advance-social-sharer` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Configure from WSS in left menu.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Should I have coding skills to use the plugin? =

No, you can use the Social Share plugin without any coding skills. It has a  user-friendly interface for installation and activation.

= I cannot see the ‘Facebook Button’ icon in the post after plugin installation. =

To resolve this issue, go to “Plugins” in the WordPress admin panel, find “Advance Social Sharer”, and click “Activate”.

= How can I deactivate the plugin? =

Go to “Plugins” in the WordPress admin panel, locate “Advance Social Sharer” and click “Deactivate”.
